What’s Special About This Drink?

Sabja Lassi brings together the richness of classic lassi and the cooling nature of sabja seeds. When soaked, sabja seeds swell and develop a soft, jelly-like texture that makes the drink both refreshing and satisfying.

This combination has long been enjoyed in traditional diets to beat the heat and provide gentle nourishment. Shake & Gulp Sabja Lassi delivers that same wholesome comfort in a convenient, ready-to-drink bottle. Since real seeds naturally settle, every bottle is meant to be shaken well before drinking.

Who Should Drink This?

Sabja Lassi is a comforting drink for everyone as part of a balanced daily routine.

It’s especially helpful for people who experience:

  • Body heat or summer fatigue
  • Dehydration during warm weather
  • A need for something soothing and filling
  • Mild digestive discomfort
  • Tiredness from long days

A gentle way to cool down and feel satisfied.
